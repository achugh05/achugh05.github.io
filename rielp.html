My AutoCad Work at PRPA

During my Prince Rupert Port Authority Internship I had the opportunity to refine my AutoCad/Civil3D skills, creating several progression maps, berm designs, an alignment profile, and a few section views. All drawings are of actual RIELP site locations, some including lidar data taken by surveyors. Feel free to zoom in or download to view details.
